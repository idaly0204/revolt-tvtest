what is Revolt-tv

Revolt-tv was founded in 2013, says its linear TV network now reaches more than 55 million homes. The company produces content and original material with the biggest names in hip-hop, along with coverage of breaking news, current events, trends and announcements within the Black cultural movement and hop-hop world.
